Who Made You Judge?

We live in a culture where opinions travel faster than understanding and condemnation often speaks louder than compassion.

Yet when we look at Jesus, we discover a Savior who never compromised truth and never abandoned mercy. He challenged hearts, confronted sin, and offered hope to those who needed it most.

This book invites readers to explore what Scripture really teaches about judgment, discernment, grace, accountability, and restoration.

About the Book

Who Made You Judge? is a thoughtful exploration of how believers can hold firmly to biblical truth while extending the grace and compassion demonstrated by Jesus Christ.

In a world filled with criticism, division, and assumptions, this book examines the difference between righteous discernment and destructive condemnation. Through Scripture, reflection, and practical application, readers are encouraged to examine their own hearts before judging others.

Rather than offering easy answers, this book points readers back to Christ and challenges them to become instruments of restoration, mercy, and truth.

About Bradley Hobbs

Bradley Hobbs is a teacher, writer, and ministry leader who is passionate about helping people grow in faith, understanding, and spiritual maturity.

Through years of teaching, mentoring, and writing, he has encouraged others to seek biblical truth while demonstrating the compassion and mercy of Christ.

Who Made You Judge? was written from a desire to help believers navigate difficult conversations with wisdom, grace, and a renewed focus on the heart of Jesus.
