About the Book

Who Made You Judge? explores the tension between truth and condemnation, grace and accountability, conviction and compassion.

Through Scripture and practical reflection, readers are challenged to consider how Jesus interacted with people while remaining faithful to truth and extending mercy.

This book invites believers to move beyond criticism and rediscover a Christ centered approach that restores rather than destroys.

Core Themes

Truth

Standing firmly on biblical truth while remaining faithful to God's Word.

Grace

Reflecting the grace of Christ in everyday relationships and conversations.

Mercy

Choosing restoration over condemnation and compassion over criticism.

Hope

Pointing people toward redemption, healing, and transformation.

About Bradley Hobbs

Bradley Hobbs is a teacher, writer, and ministry leader who is passionate about helping people grow in faith and biblical understanding.

Through writing, teaching, and encouragement, he seeks to point readers toward Jesus while addressing difficult questions with both truth and compassion.

Who Made You Judge? reflects a desire to help believers examine their own hearts while extending grace to others.
