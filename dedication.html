Dedication

With Gratitude

This book is first and foremost dedicated to my Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ, whose mercy has been greater than my failures, whose truth has never ceased to guide me, and whose grace continues to transform my life each day.

Everything within these pages exists because of His faithfulness. Every lesson learned, every truth discovered, and every word written finds its source in Him. May this book always point beyond its author and toward the One who is full of grace and truth.

To my family, thank you for your unwavering love, encouragement, patience, and support throughout this journey. Your prayers, kindness, and constant presence have strengthened me in ways that words cannot fully express.

To every teacher, pastor, mentor, friend, and believer who has invested in my spiritual growth, thank you for faithfully living the Gospel before me. Your example has helped shape both my faith and this work.

This book is also dedicated to every reader who has ever struggled beneath the weight of judgment, condemnation, shame, or regret.

Perhaps someone misunderstood your story. Perhaps harsh words left wounds that still remain. Perhaps religion felt heavier than grace. Or perhaps you have carried stones of your own and are only now discovering the freedom found in laying them down.

My prayer is that these pages remind you that Jesus Christ never stops calling people toward Himself. He meets us with truth that restores, mercy that heals, and grace that transforms.

"Blessed are the merciful: for they shall obtain mercy."

Matthew 5:7

Finally, I dedicate this book to every person who desires to reflect the heart of Christ more faithfully. May we never lose sight of the mercy that has carried us, the grace that continues to sustain us, and the Savior who invites us to walk humbly before Him.

End of Dedication
