About the Author

Bradley Hobbs

Teacher • Author • Worshipper • Follower of Jesus Christ

Bradley Hobbs is a Christian author, educator, and lifelong student of Scripture whose passion is helping people encounter the truth of God's Word through the grace and mercy revealed in Jesus Christ. His writing reflects a deep desire to encourage believers to pursue holiness without condemnation and to extend the same mercy they themselves have received.

Truth without Christ
becomes religion.
Truth in Christ
becomes redemption.

For more than six years, Bradley has served in early childhood education, investing in the lives of young children and their families while continuing to write Bible studies, devotions, and Christian books. His passion for teaching extends beyond the classroom into ministry, where he seeks to make Scripture understandable, practical, and centered on the person of Jesus Christ.

Who Made You Judge? grew out of years of personal study, prayer, and reflection. Rather than asking readers to lower biblical standards, the book invites them to rediscover the balance of truth and grace that was perfectly demonstrated in the life and ministry of Jesus.

Bradley believes that Scripture calls believers to pursue holiness with humility, conviction with compassion, and truth with love. Throughout his writing, he encourages readers to look beyond appearances, remember their own need for grace, and trust the transforming work of God in every human life.

"Walk humbly,
love mercy,
and follow Jesus."

When he is not writing, Bradley enjoys studying the Bible, leading devotions, worshipping with his church family, encouraging others through Christian ministry, and continuing to grow in his relationship with the Lord.

His prayer is that every book he writes points readers beyond the author and toward the Savior.

Soli Deo Gloria
